Arts and Design Programme

Objective of the program

To provide an opportunity for students to acquire advanced knowledge, skills and academic progression in art and design.
To provide technological and specialised skills in art and design to meet contemporary manpower needs.

Quality assurance

Takoradi Technical University has an academic quality assurance unit which oversees all systems, resources and information devoted to maintaining and improving standards and quality of teaching and learning opportunities.

About Art And Design

This programme wil prepare students for a variety of career pathways with graduates entering the servicing and manufacturing sectors of Art and Design. Therefore, after successful completion of this programme applicants can be gainfully employed.
To instill the right employment skills so that you can get a job immediately after graduation, part of the curriculum of this track is to expose you to various forms of media such as graphic designer, fashion designer, animation, photography, etc.

Major Courses

Digital Arts

At the faculty of applied arts, student is prone to the use of advanced softwares to make their works faster and easier, giving the student knowledge on art business, product design, exhibition and portfolio Review and also Aesthetics and Appreciation.

Fashion and Textiles

Fashion and textiles design is about creating a cohesive and finishing appearance that can convey sophistication, be it through clothing or interior design. Modern trend and other relevant techniques are taught. Softwares are also used.

Ceramics

Ceramic art is art from ceramic materials including clay. It may take forms including artistic pottery, tableware, tiles, figurines and other sculpture. As one of the plastic arts, ceramic art is one of the visual arts works.

Sculpture

Sculpture is the branch of visual arts that operates three dimensions. It is one of the plastic arts. Durable sculptural processes originally used carving and modelling, in stone, metal, ceramics, wood, and other materials but, since Modernism, there has been almost complete freedom of materials and process. Thus, the program provides hands on experience.

Photography

Photography is the art, application and practice of creating durable images by recording light, either electronically by means of an image sensor, or chemically by means of a light-sensitive material such as photographic film. Of which the faculty trains it students on modern trends and also helps to adapt trends in the modern world.

Academics

The Art and Design Syllabus is broad-based and seeks to develop creative practices, artistic knowledge, critical thinking and problem-solving skills in a variety of media.

FAATSA AND ITS INFLUENCE IN THE PUBLIC

At FAATSA, you can have it all: quality education and training that provides you the requisite skills employees hunt, and the chance to serve your community through internships.

Tuition

Tuition is both Online based and face to face, the student is then prone to utilize and do self own research to help broaden knowledge.

Students' Life

At FAATSA, experienced and inexperienced students come together to share ideas relating to hands on skill work and theoretical knowledge.
